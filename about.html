About Babyoid

Babyoid is an independent research initiative founded by Shambhavi Srivastavv It investigates developmental intelligence through Digital DNA, computational seeds, artificial womb architecture, and emergent evolution. Rather than designing intelligence directly, Babyoid explores whether intelligence can emerge naturally through developmental processes inspired by biological systems. The Digital Organism Architecture (DOA) serves as the theoretical foundation of the project, providing a framework for studying adaptive and self-organizing intelligence.
